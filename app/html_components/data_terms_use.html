Terms of use for the METALP data and Privacy policy

We reserve the right to not provide you the requested data if the final usage will conflict with our research or if they are still relevant for our lab ongoing research or publications. By pressing the “Send request” button you agree that you will comply with the citing instructions included in the provided file when using the downloaded data in publications.

When processing personal data, we attach great importance to protecting your private sphere and ensuring that your data are secure. By pressing the “Send request” button we will collect, process and store your personal data only as permitted by law or if you have given your consent (by accepting our terms of use). We request for information about your e-mail address, university or organisation you are currently affiliated with and a brief description of the final usage of our data. By any mean, we do not pass your data on to third parties. You may at any time request that this data is anonymized by contacting us via our METALP website.
