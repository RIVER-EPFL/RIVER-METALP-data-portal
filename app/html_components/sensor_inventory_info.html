Filter the inventory by 'Station' or 'Parameter'.

The station name must be an acronym of exactly 3 characters long.

The 'param_name' and 'serial_nb' fields can be maximum 25 characters long.

The 'param_full' and 'model' fields can be maximum 75 characters long.

The calibration field is automatically created when both the 'calibration_a' and 'calibration_b' fields are filled.

The 'param_name', 'param_full' and 'model' fields are mandatory.

The NULL keyword must be entered to explicitly delete a non-mandatory value.
